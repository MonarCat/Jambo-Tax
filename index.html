Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common tax questions in Kenya.

Personal Taxes

You can register online via the KRA iTax portal or visit a Huduma Centre. Required documents include your ID and a passport photo.

For individuals, the deadline is 30th June each year. Late filings attract penalties of up to KSh 20,000.

Business Taxes

Depending on your business type, you may owe:

  • Income Tax (Monthly/Annually)
  • VAT (If turnover > KSh 5M)
  • PAYE (If you have employees)

Penalties & Compliance

Late filings incur:

  • 5% penalty of the tax due + 1% interest per month.
  • Possible KRA audit triggers.
