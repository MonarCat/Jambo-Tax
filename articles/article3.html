Understanding the New Tax Regulations in Kenya

Published on May 3, 2025 | By Sarah Johnson (sarah.johnson@jambotax.co.ke)

1. Introduction of Digital Service Tax

The Digital Service Tax (DST) applies to income earned by non-resident companies providing digital services in Kenya. This includes streaming platforms, online marketplaces, and more. Learn more on the KRA iTax Portal.

2. Changes in VAT Rates

The VAT rate for certain goods and services has been adjusted to align with regional standards. Ensure you are aware of the updated rates to remain compliant.

3. Enhanced Compliance Measures

The Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) has introduced stricter compliance measures, including real-time monitoring of transactions through the iTax system.
